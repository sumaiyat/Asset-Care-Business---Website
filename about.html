About us

In the modern business world, the total way of operating any business has faced a drastic change. At the same time some strong visible changes affected our lifestyle and way of spending social moments. Now we always look for one point solutions, hassle free arrangements, pressure restricted logistic support and most importantly some smart and diligent people to aid us during our moments of need.

Keeping all these issues in mind back in the year 2012 we started a venture called – Asset Care. If we define Asset Care as only a business entrepreneurship, we would be wrong! Asset Care is a dream for us to realize; Asset Care is a platform for young and talented peoples around us who need the scope to show their talents, prove their capability; Asset Care a company dedicated to earn the satisfaction of the valued clients and customers by providing the best; Asset Care is a multi- directional vessel to fulfill various requirements of our clients, as deemed necessary; Asset Care is totally complaint company abiding by all the legal formalities and regulations as defined by the Government of Bangladesh.

Like any other new company we faced many challenges in our way till today. But the most important thing is we never became frustrated or demoralized, but we learnt from each of the challenges we faced; we learnt from our mistakes. Today, we have an excellent 340 member’s team of professionals, young-talented-energetic-experienced and committed to achieve the goal at any cost. We have prepared a database of individuals, logistic support providers, associates, partner organizations which could be the most valuable asset to any concern.

Our Vision


Asset Care will strive to offer excellent services by providing high quality of work, applying the latest available technology for the industry, and seeking more joint ventures with Local companies to have the opportunity in higher weighted projects. We look forward to create such a platform which will bring together young and new talents around us; culture them; cherish them and turn them into assets. We firmly believe that these young talented souls will be the light-bearers of future. They will decide our way towards success in every aspect of life.

Our Mission


To sustain the high level of qualified personnel and build a professional team committed to serve our clients. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust, through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team. We are determined to be the Market Leading light in next 05 Years.
